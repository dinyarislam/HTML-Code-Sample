Collecting coins is one of my favorite ways to spend my past time and I've been engaged with this hobby ever since I was a 10 year-old kid. I have several hundred different coins in my collection and this webiste gives a brief overview of some of the many different types of coins I have collected over the years. Most of the coins have been collected personally by me when I've travelled abroad to those countries, but there are some that I've recieved as gifts.

Based on their geographic origins, most of my coins can categorized into the following types:

  • American Coins

  • European Coins
  • Asian Coins
